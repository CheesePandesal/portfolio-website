Here's how i became hooked to coding

My journey in programming began in 2022 during my senior year in high school. While working on a research project focused on marketing and business, my group needed a website to showcase our experimental product. Since no one in our team had any programming experience, I took it upon myself to learn web development. I dove into online resources, particularly YouTube tutorials, to grasp the fundamentals of HTML and CSS. As I delved deeper, I discovered the immense potential of coding. The ability to create something useful and impactful fascinated me, and I became hooked on programming.
